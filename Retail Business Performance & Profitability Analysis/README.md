# 🛒 Retail Business Performance & Profitability Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail business performance across product categories, cities, store types, and customer behavior to identify profitability gaps, discount inefficiencies, and delivery bottlenecks. The dashboard provides insights into revenue, profit margins, return rates, discount patterns, and customer satisfaction across Indian retail markets.

The goal is to enable retail executives and operations managers to improve profitability, reduce return rates, and optimize discount and delivery strategies.

## 🎯 Business Objective

• Identify which product categories and individual products are driving or eroding profit

• Analyze how delivery performance varies across cities and its impact on customer ratings

• Compare online vs offline store performance in terms of revenue and profit

• Evaluate discount levels by category and their relationship to profitability

• Detect return rate patterns by city and category to reduce revenue leakage

## 📈 Dashboard Insights
### 1️⃣ Profitability Overview

• Total profit stands at just 2.15K on 37K revenue — a razor-thin profit margin of 5.72%

• Total cost of 35K against 37K revenue signals extremely tight operational efficiency

• Any increase in returns, discounts, or delivery costs could push the business into losses

### 2️⃣ Profit by Category

• Fashion is the only category showing a profit decrease (negative waterfall bar), making it the most concerning category

• Electronics and Grocery show positive profit contributions

• Home Appliances contributes to total profit but at a lower incremental rate

• Fashion's high discount rate of 30% is likely the primary driver of its profit erosion

### 3️⃣ Profit by Product

• Air Fryer shows a significant profit decrease — it is the single largest profit-negative product on the dashboard

• Women Kurti and Men T-Shirt are the top profit-contributing products

• Jeans, Cooking Oil, and Mixer Grinder show early-stage but growing positive contributions

• Wireless Earbuds and Smart Watch contribute moderately to overall profit

### 4️⃣ Delivery Performance by City

• Hyderabad has the worst delivery performance at 7.0 average days — nearly double that of Chennai (1.0)

• Chennai is the most delivery-efficient city at just 1.0 average day

• Delhi (3.3) and Mumbai (4.0) maintain near-average delivery performance

• Bangalore and Pune both average 5.0 days, indicating mid-tier delivery challenges

### 5️⃣ Discount & Return Rate Analysis

• Fashion carries the highest avg discount at 30%, followed by Home Appliances (22.50%), Electronics (20%), and Grocery (6.50%)

• Fashion also has the highest return rate at over 60% — the steepest of all categories

• The combination of highest discount and highest return rate in Fashion signals a structural problem — heavy discounting is not retaining customers or reducing returns

• Bangalore has a 50% return rate and Delhi has a 33% return rate, making them the highest return-risk cities

### 6️⃣ Online vs Offline Store Performance

• Online store generates significantly higher revenue (~20K) compared to Offline (~15K)

• However, profit levels between online and offline are much closer together

• This suggests offline stores may be operating with better cost control or fewer discounts despite lower revenue

### 7️⃣ Delivery Days vs Customer Rating

• Home Appliances has the highest avg delivery days (~7) and among the lowest customer ratings — a clear inverse relationship

• Grocery has the highest customer rating despite moderate delivery days, suggesting product satisfaction outweighs speed for essential items

• Fashion and Electronics show mid-range delivery times with moderate ratings

## 💡 Business Recommendations

• Urgently review the Fashion category strategy — a 30% average discount combined with a 60%+ return rate is the biggest threat to overall profitability

• Investigate and resolve Air Fryer profitability issues — it is the single largest profit-decreasing product and needs immediate pricing or cost review

• Improve delivery operations in Hyderabad — at 7 days average, it is the worst performing city and likely contributing to Bangalore's 50% return rate

• Reduce blanket discounting in Fashion and replace with targeted, loyalty-based promotions to protect margins

• Leverage the Online channel's higher revenue base by optimizing fulfillment costs to widen the online profit gap over offline

• Focus on Women Kurti and Men T-Shirt as hero products — they are the top profit contributors and should receive priority in inventory and marketing
