# 🚴 Swiggy Sales Analysis | SQL + Power BI
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Swiggy food delivery data to understand sales performance, customer ordering patterns, restaurant performance, and food preferences across cities.

The project combines SQL for data cleaning and business analysis and Power BI for interactive dashboards. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into insights that help food delivery platforms and restaurant partners make better operational and marketing decisions.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

• Analyze overall food delivery sales performance

• Identify top cities contributing to revenue

• Understand ordering patterns by day and month

• Evaluate restaurant and dish performance

• Analyze customer spending behavior and ratings

## 📈 Dashboard Insights
### 1️⃣ Sales Trend Analysis
##### Monthly Sales

• Monthly sales fluctuate between ~6.1M and ~6.8M, showing relatively stable demand with small seasonal variations.

##### Weekly Sales

• Weekly revenue remains consistent around 1.4M – 1.6M, indicating steady order flow across weeks.

##### Daily Sales Pattern

• Orders are fairly balanced across the week, with Saturday showing the highest sales volume, indicating increased weekend demand.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

#### Based on the analysis:

1️⃣ Focus on High-Demand Cities
Cities like Bengaluru and Hyderabad generate significant revenue and should remain priority markets.

2️⃣ Expand Non-Veg Menu Offerings
Non-vegetarian dishes dominate orders, presenting opportunities for menu expansion.

3️⃣ Promote Weekend Campaigns
Saturday shows higher demand, making it ideal for promotional campaigns.

4️⃣ Strengthen Partnerships with Top Restaurants
Major chains such as KFC and McDonald's drive large volumes of orders.

5️⃣ Maintain Strong Rating Quality
High ratings contribute to customer trust and repeat purchases.
