# 🍽️ Restaurant Sales & User Performance Analysis
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes restaurant sales performance across multiple cities and brands in Pakistan to identify revenue trends, customer behavior patterns, and operational gaps. The dashboard provides insights into total sales, order volumes, dish-level performance, customer segmentation, and delivery efficiency.

The goal is to enable business stakeholders to maximize revenue, optimize menu offerings, and improve delivery operations across restaurant chains.

## 🎯 Business Objective

• Identify which cities and restaurant brands are generating the highest revenue

• Analyze how customer segments (age, gender, category) drive sales performance

• Compare dish-level performance across all restaurant chains

• Evaluate delivery status efficiency and its impact on customer satisfaction

• Track sales trends over time to detect growth or decline signals

## 💡 Key Insights
### 1️⃣ City Performance

• Multan leads all cities in total sales, followed by Lahore and Peshawar

• Islamabad and Karachi contribute lower but consistent revenue, indicating untapped market potential

• Sales distribution across cities is relatively competitive with no single city dominating overwhelmingly

### 2️⃣ Restaurant & Dish Performance

• Pizza Hut ranks #1 among all restaurant chains in total sales

• Pasta is the best-selling dish at 3.08M, followed closely by Sandwich (2.97M) and Pizza (2.78M)

• Fries and Burger trail slightly but remain strong performers with 2.78M and 2.74M respectively

• Balanced dish-level revenue suggests no single-product dependency risk

### 3️⃣ Customer Behavior

• Teenagers form the largest customer segment, with Adults and Seniors nearly equal in size

• Pasta dominates dish preferences across all age groups

• Payment methods are almost evenly split — Cash (33.98%), Card (33.37%), Wallet (32.65%) — showing high digital payment readiness

• Fast Food (20.37%) and Italian (20.6%) are the top food categories by customer count

### 4️⃣ Sales Trend

• Monthly sales peaked in early 2024 at approximately 0.6M per month

• A steady declining trend is observed through mid-2025, dropping below 0.4M

• This signals a critical need for business intervention through promotions or product refresh

### 5️⃣ Delivery Performance

• Only 34.33% of orders were delivered on time

• 32.87% of orders were delayed and 32.8% were cancelled — a combined 65.67% poor delivery rate

 •Delivery inefficiency is likely a key driver behind the average rating of 3.00 out of 5

### 6️⃣ Gender-Based Sales

• Sales are fairly distributed across Male, Female, and Other gender groups

• Female customers show slightly higher sales contribution in certain restaurant chains

• Gender-targeted promotions could help increase order frequency across all segments

## 💡 Business Recommendations

• Launch city-specific campaigns in Islamabad and Karachi to close the revenue gap with top-performing cities

• Invest in Pasta and Sandwich promotions as they consistently lead in both sales and customer preference

• Introduce a delivery reliability program — reducing the 32.8% cancellation rate alone could significantly boost revenue

• Use age-based targeting — teen-focused combos and loyalty programs can capitalize on the largest customer segment

• Introduce weather and traffic-based delivery time estimates to manage customer expectations and improve ratings

• Run cashless payment incentives to accelerate the shift toward wallets and cards, reducing cash handling costs
