# 🚚 Delivery Zone Analysis Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes delivery performance across multiple customer zones to identify operational bottlenecks and improve delivery efficiency. The dashboard provides zone-wise insights into delivery time, route length, traffic conditions, weather impact, and delivery modes.

The goal is to enable operations teams to reduce delivery time and optimize routing decisions.

## 🎯 Business Objective

• Identify which customer zones have the highest delivery time

• Analyze how traffic level and weather affect delivery performance

• Compare delivery efficiency across delivery modes (Bike, Car, Scooter, Bicycle)

• Optimize route length and reduce operational delays

## 📈 Dashboard Insights
### 1️⃣ Zone Performance

• East & North zones show relatively higher order volumes.

• South zone has the highest average route length.

• West zone shows comparatively shorter routes.

### 2️⃣ Traffic Impact

• High traffic significantly increases average delivery time across all zones.

• East zone is most impacted under high traffic conditions.

### 3️⃣ Weather Impact

• Delivery time increases notably during Cloudy and Rainy conditions.

• Windy conditions impact certain zones more than others.

### 4️⃣ Delivery Mode Analysis

• Bicycle shows higher delivery time compared to motorized modes.

• Car and Scooter maintain more stable performance across traffic levels.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

• Re-optimize routing strategy in South zone to reduce route length.

• Introduce dynamic rider allocation during high traffic periods.

• Use weather-based time prediction adjustments.

• Promote motorized delivery modes in high-density zones to reduce delays.
